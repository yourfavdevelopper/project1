Who are they?

As of my last knowledge update in September 2021, Måneskin is an Italian rock band formed in Rome in 2016. The band's members are: Damiano David - Lead vocals Victoria De Angelis - Bass guitar Thomas Raggi - Guitar Ethan Torchio - Drums Key points about Måneskin: Eurovision Success: Måneskin gained international fame after winning the Eurovision Song Contest in 2021 with their song "Zitti e buoni." The band's energetic performance and rock sound garnered attention and acclaim. Musical Style: Måneskin is known for their rock and glam rock sound, drawing influences from various genres. Their music often features powerful vocals, catchy melodies, and a dynamic stage presence. Formation and Early Career: The band was formed while its members were students at the Liceo Scientifico Statale Enrico Fermi in Rome. They initially gained attention through their participation in the Italian television talent show "X Factor" in 2017, where they finished as the runner-up. Albums: Måneskin has released several albums, including "Il ballo della vita" (2018) and "Teatro d'ira: Vol. I" (2021). Their music explores a range of themes and emotions. International Recognition: Following their Eurovision win, Måneskin experienced a surge in popularity and gained fans globally. Their success marked a significant moment for Italian music on the international stage. Fashion and Image: The band is also known for its distinctive fashion sense and edgy image, contributing to their appeal among younger audiences.
