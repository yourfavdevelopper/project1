Who is Amir?

Amir Eid is an Egyptian singer, songwriter, and musician. Born on June 22, 1984, in Cairo, Egypt, he is best known as the lead vocalist of the Egyptian rock band Cairokee. Cairokee, formed in 2003, has gained significant popularity in the Middle East for its fusion of rock, alternative, and Egyptian musical influences. Amir Eid's distinctive voice and the band's socially conscious lyrics have contributed to their success. Cairokee's music often addresses social and political issues, and the band has been praised for its ability to connect with a broad audience. Some of their popular songs include "Ya El Medan" (Oh, The Square), "Sout El Horeya" (The Voice of Freedom), and "Ma A'ad Sagheran" (Never Getting Older).
