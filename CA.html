Who is Chase Atlantic ?

Chase Atlantic is an Australian alternative pop band formed in 2011. The band consists of three members: Mitchel Cave - Vocals and guitar Clinton Cave - Bass guitar and saxophone Christian Anthony - Guitar and vocals The trio originally hails from Cairns, Queensland, Australia. Chase Atlantic's music is known for its eclectic blend of genres, including pop, rock, R&B, and electronic elements. They've built a distinctive sound that incorporates moody atmospheres, catchy melodies, and a modern production style. Chase Atlantic gained attention through their independent releases and later signed with record labels. They've released several EPs and albums, including self-titled works like "Chase Atlantic" (2017) and "Phases" (2019). Some of their popular songs include "Swim," "Church," and "Friends." The band has toured internationally, and their dynamic stage presence has contributed to their growing fanbase. Chase Atlantic is known for experimenting with different musical styles and pushing boundaries within the alternative and pop genres.
