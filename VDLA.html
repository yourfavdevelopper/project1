Who is Victoria De Los Ángeles ?

De los Ángeles was particularly admired for her performances of operatic roles in works by composers such as Mozart, Verdi, Puccini, and Richard Strauss. She was also recognized for her interpretations of Spanish and French art songs. Her singing was characterized by its clarity, warmth, and emotional depth. Throughout her career, Victoria de los Ángeles collaborated with leading conductors and orchestras, and she appeared on many of the world's major opera stages. She left behind a substantial discography that includes a wide range of operatic and vocal repertoire. Her contributions to the world of classical music earned her numerous accolades and awards. Victoria de los Ángeles is remembered as one of the great sopranos of her era, and her recordings continue to be admired by opera enthusiasts and music lovers
