Who is Indila?

Indila, born Adila Sedraïa on June 26, 1984, in Paris, is a highly acclaimed French singer, songwriter, and record producer. With roots in Algerian and Indian heritage, she rose to international fame following the release of her debut album, "Mini World," in 2014. Her breakthrough came with the success of the single "Dernière Danse," a song that not only captivated French audiences but also achieved widespread popularity globally. Indila's music is characterized by a unique blend of pop, R&B, and world music influences, showcasing her versatility as an artist. The album "Mini World" received widespread critical acclaim, solidifying Indila's position as a prominent figure in the French music scene. Beyond her musical talent, she is recognized for her soulful voice and the emotional depth of her lyrics. Following the success of her debut, Indila took a hiatus from the public eye.
