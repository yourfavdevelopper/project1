Who is Stromae ?

Stromae, whose real name is Paul Van Haver, is a Belgian singer, songwriter, and musician. Born on March 12, 1985, Stromae rose to international prominence with his breakthrough single "Alors on danse" in 2009. His music is known for blending various genres, including electronic, hip-hop, and dancehall, and he often incorporates thought-provoking lyrics that address social issues. Stromae's work has been widely acclaimed for its creativity, innovative production, and poignant themes. His influence extends beyond music, as he is also known for his fashion sense and visual artistry in music videos.
