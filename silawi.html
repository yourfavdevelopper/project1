Who is Silawy?

Hossam, a 20-year-old Jordanian singer and songwriter known by the name Siilawy, has quickly gained popularity with over 2.4m YouTube subscribers and around 80 million views on his channel within a year. His soulful music and meaningful lyrics have cultivated a dedicated fan base of 3.1 million followers on Instagram. Fans describe a deep connection to Siilawy, appreciating him not just as an artist but as a relatable human being. They express being "addicted to his voice" and finding joy in his music, emphasizing the personal connection he fosters. Siilawy himself values the happiness of his fans, noting that it's essential for them to feel close to him as Hossam, his real name.
