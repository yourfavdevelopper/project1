Who is Slimane ?

Slimane, full name Slimane Nebchi, is a French singer and songwriter. He was born on October 13, 1989, in Chelles, France. Slimane gained significant recognition after winning the fifth season of "The Voice: la plus belle voix," the French edition of the television singing competition "The Voice," in 2016. After his victory on "The Voice," Slimane released his debut album titled "A Bout de Rêves" in 2016, which was well-received in France and contributed to his rising popularity. His music often incorporates elements of pop and chanson française (French song). Slimane continued to build on his success with subsequent albums, including "Solune" (2018) and "VersuS" (2019), a collaborative album with another French singer, Vitaa,chroniques d'un cupidon
