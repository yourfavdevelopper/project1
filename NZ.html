Who is Nassif?

Nassif Zeytoun is a Lebanese singer and songwriter who rose to prominence by winning the title of "Star Academy," a renowned Arabic television talent show, in 2010. Since his victory, Zeytoun has established himself as a prominent figure in the Arab music industry. Known for his distinctive voice, Nassif Zeytoun has released several highly successful songs that have enjoyed widespread popularity across the Arab world. Notable tracks in his repertoire include "Mesh Aam Tezbat Maae," "Nami Aa Sadri," and "Toul Al Yom." Zeytoun's musical style often blends modern and traditional elements, contributing to his broad appeal.
