Who are Bigflo et Oli ?

Bigflo & Oli are a French hip-hop duo consisting of two brothers, Florian "Bigflo" Ordoñez and Olivio "Oli" Ordoñez. They were born on January 22, 1993, and October 3, 1995, respectively, in Toulouse, France. The duo gained significant popularity in the French music scene for their catchy tunes, engaging lyrics, and energetic performances. Bigflo & Oli's music often addresses personal experiences, social issues, and their journey as artists. They are known for their storytelling abilities and authenticity in their lyrics. The duo's style blends elements of hip-hop, pop, and rap, making their music accessible to a diverse audience.
