Who is Joseph Attieh?

Joseph Attieh is a Lebanese singer and actor. He was born on May 14, 1986, in Batroun, Lebanon. Joseph rose to fame after winning the first season of the pan-Arab talent show "Star Academy" in 2005. This victory marked the beginning of his successful career in the music industry.
