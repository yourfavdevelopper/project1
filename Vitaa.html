Who is Vitaa ?

Vitaa, born Charlotte Gonin on March 14, 1983, in Mulhouse, France, is a French-Belgian singer and songwriter. Recognized for her soulful voice and emotive performances, Vitaa has made significant contributions to the French and Belgian music scenes, particularly in the genres of pop and R&B. Her breakthrough came with the release of her debut album "A Fleur de Toi" in 2007, featuring the hit single of the same name. Vitaa's musical journey has continued with subsequent successful albums, showcasing her ability to resonate with audiences through her vocal prowess and emotional depth. One of her notable collaborations includes the hit song "Game Over" with French rapper Maître Gims, which further solidified her presence in the music industry.
